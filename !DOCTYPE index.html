Level 2 – Beginner Trader (8–12 years old)

📈

How to Read a Chart

A chart tells the story of prices going up and down like a roller coaster. Line going up = lots of buying! Line down = wait and watch.

🕯️

Candlesticks – Traffic Lights!

Green candle = price went UP (happy day, maybe +RM50!). Red = price went DOWN. Fat part shows how much it moved. Thin lines = highest/lowest that day.

🏆

Quality Market = Happy Factory

Only buy from "happy factories" – ones making lots of money, with lots of buyers (high volume), and everyone wants their stuff!

Level 3 – Expert Trader + More Lessons

📊

How to Value a Stock

Like buying a candy factory for RM1,000 that makes RM200 profit every month – good deal! (Simple P/E ratio idea.)

🚨

When to Sell (Stop Loss & Take Profit)

If price drops 10% (lose RM100 from RM1,000), sell auto. If up 20% (gain RM200), take profit & smile!

💰

Smart Capital & Risk

Don't put all RM10,000 in one place! Risk only 1–2% (RM100–200) per trade.

📉

11. Common Beginner Mistakes

Don't chase hot tips, don't trade with money you need for school/food, don't revenge trade after a loss – stay calm!
